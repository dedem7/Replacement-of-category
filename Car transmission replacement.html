Conclusion¶

Scatter plots and cluster analysis give an evidence that technical car features and price of given dataset do not depend on transmission type. Cars with different transmissions may have similar features,including prices, and can be included in one cluster(or group).The replacement of category is possible, however it is always depends on the goal of decision maker whether the replacement for particular task will be appropriate choice or not.
